Ingredients:
 - 1 tbsp oil
 - 450g cubed chicken
 - 1 chopped fresh chili
 - 1 bag stir-fry veg
 - 1 packet baby sweetcorn & mange touts
 - 1 packet thin rice noodles
 - 1 sachet stir-fry sauce

Directions:
 1. Brown the chicken in the oil & chili.
 2. Add the stir-fry veg, sweetcorn & mange-touts.
 3. Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes
 4. Add the noodles & sauce
 5. Stir fry for 3-4 more minutes.
